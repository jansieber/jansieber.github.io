Courses

These courses offer lectures on specialised mathematical topics (see below) to PGR students at 21 UK Universities. The lectures are delivered by a member of staff from one of the participating universities. Students attend the lectures via zoom.

Credit

Each course can count toward the compulsory training if you submit and pass a pass/fail take-home exam at the end of the course. See maths-magic.ac.uk/calendar.php for exam periods.

Registration

MAGIC You will be automatically registered for MAGIC, such that you are able to login to the maths-magic.ac.uk website.

Courses You can then enrol for courses. For each course you can enrol for participation only (such that it does not count as credit) or for participation and assessment.

Hardware & software instructions

All lectures are broadcast using zoom. You can connect to the lecture using a personal account given to you when after you registered for MAGIC. The lectures require authentication such that you cannot just join by clicking on a link.

For each course you plan to attend:

  • enrol on http://maths-magic.ac.uk
  • choose if you intend to get assessed (this can be changed at a later date, before the exam period)
  • attend the lecture(s) as scheduled each week
  • after each lecture tick a box on maths-magic, confirming that you attended (for attendance monitoring)
